# Bai tap Bai 04 - Vong lap

## Muc tieu

1. Luyen `for`, `while`, `do while`.
2. Luyen tu duy lap lai va cap nhat bien dem.
3. Luyen tranh loi lap vo han.
4. Luyen `break` va `continue`.
5. Luyen giai bai toan tinh tong, dem, tim kiem bang vong lap.
6. Luyen vong lap long nhau va in mau don gian.

## Nguyen tac lam bai

1. Xac dinh bien dem bat dau.
2. Xac dinh dieu kien dung.
3. Xac dinh cach cap nhat bien dem.
4. Moi khi bi roi, hay ghi ra 3 cau hoi tren.
5. Test voi du lieu nho truoc.
6. Theo doi so lan lap bang `Console.WriteLine` neu can.

## Bai co ban

### Bai 1

Dung `for` in cac so tu 1 den 10.
Sau do doi thanh in tu 1 den 20.
Sau do doi thanh in tu 0 den 9.
Nhan xet su khac nhau giua `<=` va `<`.

### Bai 2

Dung `for` dem nguoc tu 10 ve 1.
In them dong `Bat dau` truoc vong lap.
In them dong `Ket thuc` sau vong lap.
Muc tieu la hieu trinh tu chay.

### Bai 3

Dung `for` in cac so chan tu 0 den 20.
Goi y dung `i += 2`.
Sau do thu cach khac bang `if (i % 2 == 0)`.
So sanh hai cach.

### Bai 4

Dung `while` in cac so tu 1 den 5.
Khong duoc quen tang bien dem.
Sau do thu co tinh bo dong tang bien dem xem dieu gi xay ra.
Khong chay that neu biet se lap vo han, chi mo ta.

### Bai 5

Dung `do while` in it nhat mot lan thong bao `Thu lai`.
Dat bien dem bat dau la 1.
Lap den khi bien dem > 3.
Muc tieu la thay `do while` chay mot lan truoc khi kiem tra.

### Bai 6

Dung `for` tinh tong tu 1 den 10.
In tong sau moi lan cong.
Sau do in tong cuoi cung.
Giai thich vai tro cua bien `sum`.

### Bai 7

Dung `for` tinh tong cac so le tu 1 den 19.
Sau do lam lai voi so chan tu 2 den 20.
Dat ten bien ro nghia.
Tu kiem tra ket qua bang tinh nham.

### Bai 8

In bang cuu chuong 7.
Dung `for` tu 1 den 10.
In theo mau `7 x 3 = 21`.
Sau do doi thanh bang cuu chuong 9.

### Bai 9

Dung `for` in cac so tu 1 den 10.
Khi gap so 6 thi dung vong lap bang `break`.
In thong bao ro truoc khi break.
Quan sat ket qua in ra.

### Bai 10

Dung `for` in cac so tu 1 den 7.
Bo qua so 4 bang `continue`.
In thong bao `Bo qua` tai thoi diem bo qua.
Quan sat thu tu chay.

## Bai trung cap

### Bai 11

Dung vong lap tinh giai thua cua 5.
Goi y: `1 * 2 * 3 * 4 * 5`.
In ket qua tung buoc.
Sau do thu voi 6.

### Bai 12

Dung `for` dem so chia het cho 3 trong khoang 1 den 30.
Can co bien `count`.
Moi lan tim thay so hop le thi tang bien dem.
Cuoi cung in tong so luong.

### Bai 13

Dung `for` tinh tong cac so chia het cho 5 tu 1 den 100.
Sau do tinh tong cac so khong chia het cho 5 trong cung khoang.
So sanh hai tong.
Viet ro ten bien.

### Bai 14

Dung `while` mo phong nap pin.
Pin ban dau la 0.
Moi vong tang 15.
Lap den khi pin >= 100.
In muc pin sau moi lan tang.

### Bai 15

Dung `while` dem nguoc so giay tu 5 ve 1.
Sau vong lap in `Bat dau!`.
Co the mo phong dem nguoc truoc khi choi game.
Chu y dieu kien va cap nhat.

### Bai 16

Dung `for` tim so dau tien trong khoang 50 den 100 chia het cho 9.
Khi tim thay thi in ra va `break`.
Sau do doi thanh chia het cho 11.
Giai thich vi sao break hop ly.

### Bai 17

Dung `for` duyet qua chuoi `LapTrinh`.
In ra tung ky tu va vi tri cua no.
Sau do thu in nguoc tu cuoi ve dau.
Muc tieu la hieu vong lap voi chi so chuoi.

### Bai 18

Dung `for` in hinh chu nhat 3 dong, 5 cot bang dau `*`.
Goi y dung vong lap long nhau.
Moi dong xong thi xuong dong.
Sau do doi kich thuoc thanh 4 dong, 6 cot.

### Bai 19

Dung `for` in tam giac sao tang dan 5 dong.
Dong 1 co 1 sao.
Dong 5 co 5 sao.
Sau do thu in tam giac giam dan.

### Bai 20

Dung `for` tim xem so `29` co phai so nguyen to khong.
Goi y: thu chia tu 2 den `28`.
Neu co so chia het thi ket luan khong phai nguyen to.
Dung bool de luu ket qua.

## Bai nang cao vua suc

### Bai 21

Mo phong tiet kiem.
Moi thang bo vao 500000.
Lap trong 12 thang.
In tong tien sau moi thang.
Cuoi cung in tong sau 1 nam.

### Bai 22

Mo phong level up.
Level ban dau la 1.
Moi lan lap tang 1 level.
Lap den level 10.
Neu level = 5 thi in them thong bao `Mo khoa ky nang`.

### Bai 23

Dung `for` tinh trung binh cong cua 5 so nguyen co san trong code.
Khong can nhap tu ban phim.
Co the dung 5 bien rieng hoac mot mang neu ban da biet.
Muc tieu o day la lap va cong don.

### Bai 24

Dung `while` tim boi so nho nhat cua 7 lon hon 100.
Goi y: tang dan mot bien va kiem tra `%`.
Khi tim thay thi dung vong lap.
Sau do thu voi 9 va 120.

### Bai 25

Dung `for` in cac so tu 1 den 30.
Neu chia het cho 3 thi in `Fizz`.
Neu chia het cho 5 thi in `Buzz`.
Neu chia het cho ca hai thi in `FizzBuzz`.
Nguoc lai in chinh so do.

### Bai 26

Dung `for` thong ke tong doanh thu 7 ngay.
Gan san doanh thu bang bien co san trong code.
Moi ngay in doanh thu hien tai va tong tam tinh.
Cuoi cung in tong doanh thu tuan.

### Bai 27

Dung `for` tim gia tri lon nhat trong 5 so co san.
Neu chua hoc mang, co the mo phong bang cach so sanh trong vong lap voi du lieu gia lap.
Neu da hoc mang, hay dung mang.
In ket qua cuoi cung.

### Bai 28

Dung `for` dem so ky tu hoa trong chuoi `CSharp Co Ban`.
Goi y dung dieu kien tu `A` den `Z` neu chua hoc thu vien xu ly ky tu.
Muc tieu la luyen lap voi chuoi va if.
In tong so ky tu hoa.

### Bai 29

Dung `while` mo phong rut tien ATM.
So du ban dau `1000000`.
Moi lan rut `200000`.
Lap den khi so du khong con du de rut nua.
In so du sau moi lan rut.

### Bai 30

Dung vong lap long nhau in bang cuu chuong tu 2 den 5.
Moi bang cach nhau mot dong trong.
Muc tieu la luyen doc logic hai tang lap.
In ro rang de de quan sat.

## Bai theo tinh huong that

### Bai 31

Dem so ngay tap the duc.
Trong 14 ngay, ngay chan la co tap, ngay le la nghi.
Dung vong lap de dem tong so ngay tap.
In ra lich don gian.

### Bai 32

Mo phong he thong nhac nuoc.
Cu sau 2 gio hien mot thong bao.
Trong 12 gio lam viec, co bao nhieu lan nhac?
Dung vong lap phu hop.
In ro thoi diem nhac.

### Bai 33

Mo phong danh sach bai hoc.
Co 8 bai.
Da hoan thanh bai 1, 2, 3, 5.
Dung vong lap in trang thai tung bai.
Neu chua hoc thi in `Can hoc`.

### Bai 34

Mo phong don hang bi loi.
Co 10 don, trong do don so 4 va 8 loi.
Dung vong lap duyet qua tat ca don.
Gap don loi thi bo qua bang `continue`.
In cac don duoc xu ly thanh cong.

### Bai 35

Mo phong tim mat khau dung trong toi da 5 lan thu.
Khong can nhap that, co the gia lap lan thu dung la lan 4.
Dung vong lap va `break`.
In so lan da thu.

## Goi y

1. Neu bai co so lan lap ro rang, uu tien `for`.
2. Neu bai lap den khi dat trang thai nao do, uu tien `while`.
3. Neu can chay it nhat mot lan, nghi den `do while`.
4. Luon kiem tra bien dem co thay doi khong.
5. Luon kiem tra dieu kien dung co hop ly khong.
6. Hay test voi tap du lieu nho truoc.
7. Khi in mau, hay nghi theo dong va cot.
8. Khi tinh tong, luon co bien tong khoi tao truoc vong lap.
9. Khi dem so luong, luon co bien dem khoi tao tu 0.
10. Khi tim kiem, can suy nghi xem co nen `break` khi thay ket qua khong.

## Xu ly loi thuong gap

1. Loi: lap vo han.
Nguyen nhan: quen cap nhat bien dem hoac dieu kien khong bao gio sai.
Huong sua: xem lai gia tri bien dem moi vong.

2. Loi: thieu hoac thua mot lan lap.
Nguyen nhan: nham `<` va `<=`.
Huong sua: liet ke day gia tri tren giay.

3. Loi: break lam mat du lieu can xu ly.
Nguyen nhan: dung break qua som.
Huong sua: chi break khi da chac chan khong can lap tiep.

4. Loi: continue lam bo qua logic can co.
Nguyen nhan: dat continue truoc khi cap nhat hay truoc khi in can thiet.
Huong sua: kiem tra lai thu tu lenh trong than vong lap.

## Tu kiem tra

1. Ban co phan biet duoc `for` va `while` khong?
2. Ban co biet khi nao dung `do while` khong?
3. Ban co nho cach tranh lap vo han khong?
4. Ban co hieu `break` va `continue` khac nhau nhu the nao khong?
5. Ban co tu in duoc tam giac sao bang vong lap long nhau khong?
6. Ban co giai duoc bai tinh tong va bai tim kiem bang vong lap khong?

## Tong ket

1. Vong lap la mot trong nhung cong cu quan trong nhat cua lap trinh.
2. Nam chac vong lap se giup ban hoc mang, chuoi, va thuat toan de hon.
3. Moi bai tap nen duoc thu voi nhieu gia tri khac nhau.
4. Co gang doan so lan lap truoc khi chay se giup ban tien bo rat nhanh.
