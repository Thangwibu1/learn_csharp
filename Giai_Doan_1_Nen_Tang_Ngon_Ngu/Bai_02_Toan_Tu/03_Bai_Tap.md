# Bai tap Bai 02 - Toan tu

## Muc tieu bai tap

1. Luyen cong, tru, nhan, chia, chia lay du.
2. Luyen toan tu so sanh va toan tu logic.
3. Luyen cach tach bien trung gian de code de doc.
4. Luyen cach doan ket qua truoc khi chay chuong trinh.
5. Luyen cach phan biet `=` va `==`.
6. Luyen su dung ngoac de kiem soat thu tu uu tien.
7. Luyen viet bai toan thuc te bang phep tinh don gian.
8. Luyen cach bien doi mot de bai thanh bien dau vao va cong thuc.

## Cach hoc de dat hieu qua cao

1. Moi bai hay viet bien bang ten co nghia.
2. Moi bai hay ghi chu du doan ket qua vao comment truoc khi chay.
3. Sau khi chay, so sanh ket qua that voi ket qua du doan.
4. Neu sai, hay tim xem sai do nham cong thuc hay nham kieu du lieu.
5. Thu doi gia tri dau vao it nhat 3 lan cho moi bai.
6. Neu thay bieu thuc dai, hay tach thanh nhieu bien nho.
7. Neu thay kho doc, hay them ngoac tron.
8. Neu thay ket qua la bool, hay doc no thanh cau hoi dung sai.

## Bai co ban

### Bai 1

Khai bao hai bien `a = 14` va `b = 5`.
In ra ket qua cua cac phep tinh sau.
`a + b`
`a - b`
`a * b`
`a / b`
`a % b`

### Bai 2

Khai bao `width = 8` va `height = 6`.
Tinh dien tich hinh chu nhat.
Tinh chu vi hinh chu nhat.
In ro tung bien trung gian.
Thu doi `width` va `height` thanh gia tri khac.

### Bai 3

Khai bao `gold = 200`.
Mua kiem gia `75`.
Mua hai binh mau, moi binh gia `20`.
Tinh tong chi phi.
Tinh so vang con lai.

### Bai 4

Khai bao `score = 50`.
Cong them `15` diem bang toan tu `+=`.
Tru di `10` diem bang toan tu `-=`.
Nhan doi diem bang `*=`.
Cuoi cung chia cho `5` bang `/=`.

### Bai 5

Khai bao `playerLevel = 1`.
Tang level 4 lan bang `++`.
Giam 1 lan bang `--`.
In ket qua sau moi lan thay doi.
Tu mo ta su khac nhau giua viet tach dong va viet trong mot bieu thuc.

### Bai 6

Khai bao `currentHp = 45` va `maxHp = 100`.
Kiem tra `currentHp == maxHp`.
Kiem tra `currentHp < maxHp`.
Kiem tra `currentHp > 0`.
Kiem tra `currentHp <= 45`.

### Bai 7

Khai bao `age = 17`.
Kiem tra `age >= 18`.
Kiem tra `age < 18`.
Kiem tra `age != 20`.
Sau do doi `age = 20` va chay lai.

### Bai 8

Khai bao `hasKey = true` va `isNearDoor = false`.
Tinh bien `canOpenDoor = hasKey && isNearDoor`.
Tinh bien `canTryDoor = hasKey || isNearDoor`.
Tinh bien `isBlocked = !isNearDoor`.
In ro ket qua cua tung bieu thuc.

### Bai 9

Khai bao `gold = 120`, `level = 8`, `hasTicket = true`.
Kiem tra nguoi choi co du dieu kien vao phu ban khong.
Dieu kien la `gold >= 100 && level >= 8 && hasTicket`.
Thu doi tung bien mot lan de xem ket qua thay doi ra sao.
Ghi lai nhan xet ngan.

### Bai 10

Khai bao `temperature = 31`.
Dung toan tu ba ngoi de tao bien `message`.
Neu nhiet do lon hon hoac bang 30 thi la `Troi nong`.
Nguoc lai la `Troi mat`.
In bien `message`.

### Bai 11

Khai bao `x = 2`, `y = 3`, `z = 4`.
Tinh `x + y * z`.
Tinh `(x + y) * z`.
Tinh `x * y + z`.
Tinh `x * (y + z)`.
Tu giai thich vi sao ket qua khac nhau.

### Bai 12

Khai bao `double total = 7` va `double count = 2`.
Tinh trung binh `total / count`.
Sau do khai bao `int total2 = 7` va `int count2 = 2`.
Tinh trung binh lan hai.
So sanh hai ket qua va giai thich.

### Bai 13

Khai bao `minutes = 135`.
Tinh so gio bang phep chia nguyen.
Tinh so phut le bang phep chia lay du.
In theo mau `2 gio 15 phut`.
Thu them voi `minutes = 59`.

### Bai 14

Khai bao `students = 53`.
Moi nhom co `6` nguoi.
Tinh so nhom day.
Tinh so hoc vien con du.
Dat ten bien sao cho khong can doc comment van hieu.

### Bai 15

Khai bao `price = 450000`.
Khai bao `discountRate = 0.15`.
Tinh so tien giam.
Tinh gia sau giam.
In day du ro rang tung gia tri.

## Bai trung cap

### Bai 16

Tinh tien dien nuoc cua mot thang gia lap.
Khai bao `oldIndex = 1250` va `newIndex = 1388`.
Tinh so don vi tieu thu.
Gia moi don vi la `3500`.
Tinh tong tien.

### Bai 17

Khai bao `salary = 12000000`.
Thue suat la `10%`.
Tinh thue phai nop.
Tinh luong thuc nhan.
Dung ten bien ro nghia.

### Bai 18

Khai bao `distance = 150` va `time = 3`.
Tinh toc do trung binh.
Kiem tra toc do co lon hon `40` khong.
Luu ket qua kiem tra vao bien bool.
In ro tat ca bien.

### Bai 19

Khai bao `completed = 17` va `total = 20`.
Tinh phan tram hoan thanh.
Kiem tra xem da dat it nhat `80%` chua.
Neu can, dung kieu `double` de ket qua chinh xac hon.
Giai thich vi sao khong nen dung int trong cong thuc nay neu can so le.

### Bai 20

Khai bao `cartItem1 = 120000`, `cartItem2 = 85000`, `cartItem3 = 46000`.
Tinh tong tam tinh.
Neu tong lon hon hoac bang `200000` thi giam `30000` phi van chuyen.
Tinh tong thanh toan sau cung.
Dung bien bool de luu trang thai duoc mien phi ship hay khong.

### Bai 21

Khai bao `bossHp = 725`.
Moi lan danh gay `90` sat thuong.
Tinh so lan danh toi thieu de ha boss bang cach tu duy toan hoc.
Goi y: dung chia va phan du.
In ra so lan danh can thiet.

### Bai 22

Khai bao `coin = 987`.
Mot vat pham gia `120` coin.
Tinh so vat pham mua duoc toi da.
Tinh so coin con lai.
Sau do thu doi gia vat pham thanh `75`.

### Bai 23

Khai bao `passedTheory = true`, `passedPractice = false`, `paidFee = true`.
Kiem tra hoc vien co du dieu kien nhan chung chi khong.
Dung toan tu logic de bieu dien luat.
Thu doi `passedPractice = true` va chay lai.
Mo ta ket qua bang loi.

### Bai 24

Khai bao `inventorySlots = 30` va `usedSlots = 27`.
Tinh `freeSlots`.
Kiem tra tui co day khong.
Kiem tra con it hon `5` o trong khong.
In ra hai bien bool khac nhau.

### Bai 25

Khai bao `hour = 21`.
Dung toan tu ba ngoi de tao bien `greeting`.
Neu `hour < 12` thi `Chao buoi sang`.
Neu khong, tam thoi cho `Khong phai buoi sang`.
Sau do mo rong bai nay bang if else neu muon.

## Bai nang cao vua suc

### Bai 26

Tinh tong tien tra gop gia lap.
Gia san pham la `2400000`.
Tra truoc `30%`.
So con lai chia deu cho `6` thang.
Tinh tien tra truoc va tien moi thang.

### Bai 27

Khai bao `online = true`, `hasPermission = true`, `isBanned = false`.
Kiem tra nguoi dung co duoc post bai khong.
Goi y: phai online, co quyen, va khong bi khoa.
In ket qua cuoi cung.
Tu dat them 2 truong hop thu nghiem khac.

### Bai 28

Khai bao `correctAnswers = 37` va `totalQuestions = 50`.
Tinh ti le dung theo phan tram.
Kiem tra co dat moc `70%` khong.
Kiem tra co dat moc `90%` khong.
In ket qua ro rang.

### Bai 29

Khai bao `month = 14`.
Tinh nam va thang con lai tu tong so thang.
Goi y: dung `/` va `%`.
In theo mau `1 nam 2 thang`.
Thu them voi `month = 25`.

### Bai 30

Khai bao `dataUsed = 3875` MB.
Goi cuoc cho phep `4096` MB.
Tinh dung luong con lai.

Kiem tra co con it hon `500` MB khong.

### Bai 31

Khai bao `criticalMultiplier = 1.5`.

### Bai 32

Khai bao `steps = 8650`.
Muc tieu la `10000`.

### Bai 33

Khai bao `totalOrders = 128`.
Muc tieu tuan la `150`.

### Bai 34

Khai bao `battery = 18`.
Khai bao `isCharging = false`.
Kiem tra co can hien canh bao pin yeu khong.
Dieu kien: pin duoi hoac bang `20` va khong dang sac.
In ket qua bool.

### Bai 35

Khai bao `bookPages = 275`.
Moi ngay doc `18` trang.
Tinh so ngay doc tron ven.
Tinh so trang con lai cua ngay cuoi.

### Bai 39

## Goi y

1. Luon tach de bai thanh bien dau vao.
2. Sau do viet cong thuc bang toan tu.
3. Cuoi cung moi in ket qua.
4. Khong nen viet mot dong qua dai neu moi hoc.
5. Hay dat ten bien co nghia thay vi `a`, `b`, `c`.
6. Khi thay sai ket qua, kiem tra lai kieu du lieu truoc.
7. Kiem tra lai thu tu uu tien.
8. Kiem tra lai co quen ngoac hay khong.
9. Kiem tra lai co nham `=` thanh `==` hay khong.
10. Kiem tra lai co chia so nguyen khi can so thuc hay khong.
11. Kiem tra lai co cong chuoi sai cach hay khong.
12. Kiem tra lai co tinh nham tong chi phi hay khong.
13. Moi bai nen thu it nhat 3 bo du lieu.
14. Thu voi gia tri nho, gia tri lon, va gia tri bien.
15. Gia tri bien la gia tri o sat moc dieu kien.
16. Vi du `gold = 99`, `100`, `101`.
17. Vi du `battery = 20`, `21`, `19`.
18. Day la cach test canh bien rat huu ich.
19. Neu bai toan co phan tram, can than voi int.
20. Neu bai toan co tien, uu tien decimal trong du an that.

## Xu ly loi thuong gap

1. Loi: ket qua chia ra bi mat phan le.
Nguyen nhan: dung int thay vi double.
Huong sua: doi mot trong hai toan hang sang double.

2. Loi: ket qua cong va noi chuoi bi sai.
Nguyen nhan: quen ngoac quanh phep tinh.
Huong sua: viet `"Tong: " + (a + b)`.

3. Loi: dieu kien luon sai.
Nguyen nhan: viet `=` trong khi can `==`.
Huong sua: doi lai toan tu dung muc dich.

4. Loi: kho doc bieu thuc bool dai.
Nguyen nhan: nhoi qua nhieu dieu kien trong mot dong.
Huong sua: tach thanh bien bool trung gian.

5. Loi: nham thu tu uu tien.
Nguyen nhan: nghi rang cong duoc tinh truoc nhan.
Huong sua: hoc lai quy tac va them ngoac.

6. Loi: bai toan dung duoc nhung kho bao tri.
Nguyen nhan: ten bien khong ro nghia.
Huong sua: doi ten bien theo noi dung thuc te.

## Tu kiem tra

1. Ban co phan biet duoc `=` va `==` khong?
2. Ban co nho khi nao dung `%` khong?
3. Ban co biet vi sao `7 / 2` ra `3` voi int khong?
4. Ban co doc duoc bieu thuc bool thanh cau noi khong?
5. Ban co nho `&&`, `||`, `!` khac nhau ra sao khong?
6. Ban co biet khi nao dung toan tu ba ngoi khong?
7. Ban co biet them ngoac khi can de ro nghia khong?
8. Ban co biet cach test bang cac gia tri canh bien khong?
9. Ban co the giai thich vi sao ten bien quan trong khong?
10. Ban co the tu viet them 5 bai toan thuc te nua khong?

## Tong ket

1. Bai toan tu la nen tang cua moi bai lap trinh co ban.
2. Neu bai nay vung, ban se hoc if else va vong lap de hon.
3. Moi bai tap nen co du doan truoc khi chay.
4. Moi ket qua sai deu la mot co hoi hieu sau hon.
5. Hay tu viet them tinh huong gan cuoc song cua ban.
